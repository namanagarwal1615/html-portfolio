The Best Movies According to Naman Agarwal

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Three Idiots

Based on book Five Point Someone. Story of 3 friends and positive outlook of one friend towards life.

Andhadhun

A piano player pretending to be visually-impaired, unwittingly becomes entangled in a number of problems as he witnesses the murder of a former film actor.

Taare Zameen Par

A student is criticised by his parents for his poor academic performance and is sent away to a boarding school. However, his art teacher realises he has dyslexia and helps him uncover his potential.
